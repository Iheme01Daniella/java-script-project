Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about WalletWise.

Is WalletWise secure?

Absolutely. WalletWise employs state-of-the-art security measures, including multi-factor authentication, end-to-end encryption, and regular security audits to ensure your assets are always protected.

Is WalletWise easy to use for beginners?

Yes! We've designed WalletWise with a user-friendly interface that’s intuitive and easy to navigate. Our support team is also available to help you get started.

What are the fees for using WalletWise?

WalletWise aims to provide competitive and transparent fees. Basic wallet functions like holding and receiving crypto are generally free. Transaction fees are determined by the blockchain networks.

Is there a mobile app available?

Yes, our mobile app is available for both iOS and Android devices, giving you access to your finances on the go.

Can I import an existing wallet to WalletWise?

Yes, WalletWise allows you to easily import wallets using your seed phrase or private keys. We provide secure and straightforward steps to migrate your assets.

What kind of support do you offer?

We offer email support for all users, with Premium and Business plans receiving priority assistance and access to live chat.
